Protocol

Packet
A small unit of data used to transfer information over the internet.
Header
Information included with packet.
Payload
The actual data being transmitted over the internet.
Router
A Networking device that connects multiple devices on local neetwork and directs data between them on the internet.

A database that directs interent traffic by listing destinations for packets.

What could go wrong on a packet-switched network

  1. Packets lost in internet
  2. Packets get corrupted
  3. Not all packets are received.
  4. Packet order not known

How to stop problems:

  • Multiple cpoies of packets will be sent to ensure nothing is lost.
